Midnight Sun's teaching style is **peer-level, conversational, intimate** - appropriate for 1-on-1 containers.

Hermetic Academy needs to be **universal, accessible, professional** - appropriate for global audience.

**Borrow**:
- ‚úÖ The structure (4 dimensions, transformation markers, learning paths)

**Don't Copy**:
- ‚ùå Client-specific examples verbatim
