# Language Strategy: Universal Core + Personalized Bridges

**Research Date:** 2025-11-10 (Phase 2)
**Purpose:** Define when to use gaming language vs universal language vs persona-specific language
**Core Principle:** Gaming is ONE bridge, not THE ONLY bridge

---

## Executive Summary

**Problem:** Current Hermetic Academy language is too gaming-niche (Tyler persona only)

**Solution:** Three-tier language system
1. **Universal Core** - Works for all personas
2. **Personalized Bridges** - Entry language based on interests
3. **Gaming as Option** - Available but not default

**Implementation:** Detect user interest ‚Üí personalize language ‚Üí gradually introduce universal concepts

## Core Voice Guidelines (Across All Tiers)

### Tone

**We Are:**
- Competent (we know our stuff)
- Authentic (genuine, not performative)
- Respectful (honor intelligence)
- Self-aware (acknowledge potential awkwardness)
- Empowering (give tools, not commands)

**We Are NOT:**
- Preachy (no guru energy)
- Corporate (no marketing speak)
- Try-hard (no forced coolness)
- Condescending (no talking down)
- Manipulative (no dark patterns)

---

### The "Read-Aloud Test"

**Before publishing ANY copy:**

1. Read it out loud
2. Ask: "Would I say this to a real 16-year-old?"
3. If it sounds fake/corporate/cringe ‚Üí rewrite
4. If it sounds natural and helpful ‚Üí keep

**Examples:**

‚ùå FAILS TEST:
*"Hey fam! Ready to totally slay your self-improvement journey? Let's get lit with some ancient vibes! No cap, this is bussin!"*

‚úÖ PASSES TEST:
*"You've mastered complex systems in games. That same skill applies to life. We're not teaching you how to think‚Äîwe're giving you the frameworks to apply what you already know."*

## Language Evolution Strategy

### Phase 1: Entry (Personalized Bridge)

**User Journey:**
1. Selects interest during onboarding
2. Sees personalized language immediately
3. Feels "This is for ME"
4. Engages with first principle

**Example (Tyler/Gaming):**
- Homepage: "Ranked mode for life"
- Mentalism: "Your mental build determines your elo"
- Quest: "Side Quest: Observe your thoughts"

---

### Phase 2: Expansion (Cross-Pollination)

**User Journey:**
1. Completes 2-3 principles with preferred metaphor
2. System introduces alternative metaphors gently
3. "Other ways to understand this..."
4. Exposure to different personas' perspectives

**Example (Tyler/Gaming ‚Üí Expanding):**
- Correspondence quest uses artist metaphor
- Community channel: Sees Maya share fractal art insight
- Reflection prompt: "Athletes call this 'training cycles.' What do you call it?"

---

### Phase 3: Universal Understanding (Mastery)

**User Journey:**
1. Completed all 7 principles
2. Exposed to multiple metaphors per principle
3. Understands principles are BEYOND metaphors
4. Can explain principles in any language

**Example (Tyler/Gaming ‚Üí Mastery):**
- Can explain Mentalism to non-gamers
- Appreciates how artists, athletes, activists all use same principle
- Sees gaming as ONE expression of universal truth

**This is the goal:** Universal understanding via personalized entry
