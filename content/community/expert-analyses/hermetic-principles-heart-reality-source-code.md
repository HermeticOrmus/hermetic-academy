- Misaligned: Judging "other" as less-than

**Vibration** (Everything moves):
- Heart check: What frequency am I vibrating?
- Aligned: Love, truth, service, creativity
- Misaligned: Fear, manipulation, extraction, control

**Polarity** (Opposites are one):
- Heart check: Am I integrating or at war?
- Aligned: Holding both poles with compassion
- Misaligned: Identifying with one, demonizing other

**Rhythm** (Everything flows):
- Heart check: Am I honoring natural cycles?
- Aligned: Releasing what wants to complete
- Misaligned: Clinging, forcing, resisting flow

**Cause & Effect** (Every action has consequence):
- Heart check: Can I accept all consequences?
- Aligned: Acting with full responsibility
- Misaligned: Wanting results without owning effects

**Gender** (Masculine/feminine balance):
- Heart check: Am I balanced or over-identified?
- Aligned: Integrating both energies
- Misaligned: Rejecting or over-expressing one

**The simplest practice**:

- Breathe deeply

- Listen for the answer (not ego's voice)
